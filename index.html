Electricity Consumption Classifier

This page helps classify electricity consumption based on the kilowatt-hour (kWh) usage calculated by the user.





Enter your monthly electricity consumption in kWh and click the button to find out your consumption category.

Category Usage (kWh) Description
Lifeline Consumer 0 - 100 Discounted electricity rates
Low Consumption 101 - 200 Normal residential rate
Average Consumption 201 - 300 Typical electricity usage
High Consumption 301 - 500 Higher electricity usage
Very High Consumption Above 500 Heavy electricity users
